CEO Message

President & CEO

Toshiro ADEGAWA

We provide a full range of services, from planning and software development to video and audio production.
Each division is staffed with experienced professionals,
and our commitment to quality has earned us strong trust from major manufacturers.

The high standards of Alex’s products and our rapid growth are recognized throughout the industry.

Although we have achieved much, there is still more we aim to accomplish —
and strengths that only Alex can deliver.

Our achievements give us confidence,
and infinite possibilities still lie ahead.

In Japan, many companies have continued for more than a century.
To carry Alex’s technical excellence forward into the next hundred years,
we will continue building a company that is truly valued by society — together with our team.
