Video Production
Department 3D Designer

Mr O.A

Please tell us about your current job.

As a 3D designer, I create 3D materials related to video.

Currently, I'm creating 3D models for the reel frames used in slot video. The tools I mainly use are

Maya and After Effects.

It's a delicate and difficult job, as the impression of the work changes dramatically depending on the lighting and the viewing angle, but since Scott's reel frames are what appear on screen for the longest time, I find creating them very rewarding.

What is rewarding and appealing about your job?

One of the attractions of this job is being able to constantly feel my own growth.

I've only just started, but I've had many opportunities to repeatedly use the knowledge I've been taught, and before I knew it, it was naturally incorporated into my work. I also have many opportunities to gain new knowledge, and each time I do, the range of expression I can create expands, making it a lot of fun.

Another thing is that when I imagine the work I'm in charge of being released to the world and played by actual users, I become even more engaged in my work. I've often been captivated by the visuals when playing gaming machines, so I find it very rewarding to be involved in video production that can entertain users in the same way.

What is the appeal of ALEX?

I've only just joined the company, but what I've felt while working here is that this is a company that really values ​​its employees.

I'm still inexperienced both as an engineer and as a member of society, but my seniors give me very careful guidance and I feel like I'm growing every day.

The atmosphere in the company is great and there are often lively conversations, so I really enjoy coming to work. Furthermore, they are willing to listen sincerely to any concerns I may have, so I feel comfortable working here.

What are your future goals and challenges?

My future goal is to be able to quickly turn what I want to create into reality. Right now I'm not very confident about using the software, so I often have to ask for instruction, but I would like to grow into a creator that people can trust and be entrusted with work.

I also hope to become a fully-fledged member of society, not just as a creator, so that I can contribute to the company, my seniors, and the juniors I may have in the future.

Message for those currently looking for work and those planning to join the company

This company offers a great environment for people who love videos and gaming machines, and who enjoy a sense of growth.

Thanks to the careful guidance of our senior employees, there is a lot of rewarding work regardless of your position, so if you work hard, I think you'll have fun improving your skills.

What's more, the important interpersonal relationships in the workplace are not bad, so I hope you'll feel comfortable joining us.
